# SEO Strategy: Rank as Best BDC for Used Automobiles (US / Global)

**Goal:** When people search "best BDC company," "BDC for used cars," "automotive BDC USA," or "BDC services" — Smart BDC should appear at the top (US and globally where relevant).

---

## 1. Target Keywords (what people actually search)

| Intent | Keywords to own |
|--------|------------------|
| **Brand / best** | best BDC company, best BDC for dealerships, top BDC company USA, #1 BDC for auto dealers |
| **Service + vehicle** | BDC for used cars, BDC for used car dealerships, automotive BDC services, car dealership BDC |
| **Generic** | BDC services, outsourced BDC, dealership BDC, what is a BDC, BDC company |
| **Geo** | BDC company USA, BDC services United States, automotive BDC nationwide, global BDC |

---

## 2. On-Site (what we can control on the website)

### 2.1 Title and meta (already improved in this pass)
- **Homepage:** Include "Best BDC" or "BDC for Dealerships" + "US" or "Global" in title and description.
- **Automotive page:** Include "Best BDC for Used Cars" / "Automotive BDC USA" in title and description.
- Keep titles under ~60 characters, descriptions under ~155 so they don’t get cut off in search results.

### 2.2 Structured data (schema)
- **Organization** — You have it. Add **WebSite** with `potentialAction` (SearchAction or “Request Demo”) so Google can show sitelinks and associate the brand with the domain.
- **Service** — Keep on automotive and other service pages.
- **FAQPage** — Add on homepage or a dedicated FAQ page for queries like “What is a BDC?”, “Who is the best BDC company?” to target featured snippets.
- **LocalBusiness** (optional) — If you add a US address, add LocalBusiness so you can show up for “BDC company near me” / local pack.

### 2.3 Content that ranks
- **Dedicated “Best BDC” or “Why Smart BDC” page** — One page that clearly uses “best BDC for used car dealerships,” “top BDC company,” etc. in H1, H2s, and body. Link from homepage and nav/footer.
- **Blog / resources** — Articles like “Best BDC Practices for Used Car Dealerships,” “What to Look for in an Automotive BDC,” “BDC for Used Cars: Complete Guide.” Each targets long-tail keywords and brings in backlinks and internal links.
- **FAQ section** — On homepage or its own page: “What is a BDC?”, “What does a BDC do?”, “Best BDC company for used cars?” with FAQPage schema for featured snippets.
- **Used cars explicitly** — Use “used car dealerships,” “new and used car dealers” in copy on homepage and automotive page so you rank for “BDC for used cars.”

### 2.4 Internal linking
- Link from homepage, Services, and Why Us to the **automotive BDC** page with anchor text like “best BDC for used car dealerships,” “automotive BDC services.”
- Add **industry pages** (automotive, solar, RV, etc.) to sitemap and link to them from relevant content.

### 2.5 Sitemap and crawlability
- **Sitemap:** Include all important URLs (homepage, services, automotive-bdc, other industry pages, contact, why-us). Already updated in this pass.
- **robots.txt:** Keep Allow: / and Sitemap. No need to block anything unless you have a reason.

## 4. Off-Site (what you do outside the website)

These matter as much or more than on-site for “best” and “top” queries.

### 4.1 Backlinks
- Get links from **automotive and dealer industry** sites: NADA, dealer associations, state dealer boards, automotive blogs, CRM/lead-gen partners.
- **PR:** “Smart BDC named top BDC provider,” “Best BDC for used car dealers” in press releases and syndication.
- **Partners:** Ask CRM vendors (VinSolutions, CDK, etc.), dealer groups, or agencies to link to you as a recommended BDC.

### 4.2 Citations and directories
- **Google Business Profile** — Create/claim with category like “Business development service” or “Marketing agency.” Add service area (USA or nationwide). Use “best BDC for used car dealerships” in description.
- **Bing Places**, **Apple Maps** — Same idea.
- **Industry directories** — Any “best BDC companies,” “dealer services,” or “automotive outsourcing” lists. Get listed and linked.

### 4.3 Reviews and reputation
- Encourage **Google reviews** (and others) from dealers. “Best BDC” often correlates with review count and rating.
- Use **schema** (Organization or LocalBusiness) so review stars can show in search when you have them.

### 4.4 Brand searches
- The more people search **“Smart BDC”** and click your site, the stronger the association. Use consistent name everywhere (site, social, PR, partners).
- **Social profiles** — LinkedIn, Facebook, etc. with same name and link to smartbdcltd.com. Add URLs to Organization `sameAs` in schema.

## 6. Why “when someone searches BDC it should be my brand”

- **Generic “BDC”** is hard to own (one word, broad). You’ll rank better for:
  - **Smart BDC** (brand).
  - **Best BDC company**, **best BDC for used cars**, **automotive BDC services**, **BDC for dealerships** (service + intent).
- Combine **on-site** (titles, meta, schema, content, internal links) with **off-site** (links, citations, reviews, brand searches). Doing both gives you the best chance to appear at the top in the US and globally where you serve.

Use this doc as the master plan; implement the checklist and off-site steps over the next 3–6 months for lasting results.
